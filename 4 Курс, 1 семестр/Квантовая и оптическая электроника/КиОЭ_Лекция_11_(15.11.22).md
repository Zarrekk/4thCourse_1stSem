$Q_p=\frac {\omega_p}{\Delta\omega_p}$ ; $Q_p \equiv 2\pi \frac {U_{\lambda}^\sum}{U_{\lambda_{потери}}}$

$$Q= \omega_{\lambda}\frac {U_{\lambda}^\sum}{|\frac {dU_{\lambda_{потери}}}{dt}|} \Rightarrow \frac {dU_{\lambda}}{dt}= -\frac {\omega}{Q}U_{\lambda}$$
